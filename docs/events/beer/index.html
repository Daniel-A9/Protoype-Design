About This Event

Join us for an exclusive summer craft beer tasting featuring the finest seasonal brews from local and regional breweries. This intimate event will showcase limited-edition summer releases, seasonal IPAs, refreshing wheat beers, and unique fruit-infused ales.

Our expert brewers have carefully curated a selection of exceptional craft beers that capture the essence of summer. From crisp lagers to bold stouts, each beer tells a story of craftsmanship and passion. You'll discover new flavors, learn about brewing techniques, and connect with fellow beer enthusiasts in a relaxed, welcoming atmosphere.

This event is perfect for both seasoned beer connoisseurs and those just beginning their craft beer journey. Our knowledgeable staff will guide you through each tasting, explaining the unique characteristics of each brew, from the hop varieties used to the brewing process that creates these distinctive flavors.

What's Included

  • Tasting of 8 different craft beers
  • Expert guidance from professional brewers
  • Light appetizers and snacks
  • Take-home tasting notes and beer guide
  • Networking with fellow beer enthusiasts
