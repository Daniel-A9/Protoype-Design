About This Event

The Summer Music Festival brings together emerging artists, national touring acts, and local vendors for three unforgettable nights along the Austin riverfront. Enjoy indie, folk, and electronic performances across two stages while sampling Texas-inspired dishes from curated food trucks and sipping craft beverages in the shaded lounge.

Between sets, explore an artisan marketplace featuring handmade goods, vinyl pop-ups, and interactive art installations. Families are welcome during daylight hours with a dedicated kids’ zone, instrument petting zoo, and storytelling sessions from community partners.

Whether you’re here for the headliners or to discover the next hometown favorite, the festival pairs a laid-back Hill Country vibe with top-tier production, immersive lighting, and river breezes that make August nights feel magical.

What's Included

  • Access to 30+ live performances across two outdoor stages
  • Complimentary water stations and cooling lounges
  • Nightly light show finale over the river
  • Entry to the artisan marketplace and interactive art zone
  • Discount codes from partnering record stores and studios
