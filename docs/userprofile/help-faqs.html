Help Message Center / FAQs

Need help? Send us a message.

We're committed to great customer service, but due to limited staff we kindly ask that you message us with any issues before calling.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I create an account?
To create an account, click on the "Sign Up" button in the top right corner of the homepage. You can sign up using your email address, Google account, or other social media accounts. Follow the prompts to complete your profile setup.
How do I place an order?
Browse our marketplace to find products you're interested in. Click on a product to view details, then click "Add to Cart" or "Buy Now". Review your cart and proceed to checkout where you'll enter your shipping information and payment details.
What payment methods do you accept?
We accept major credit cards (Visa, Mastercard, American Express), debit cards, PayPal, and other digital payment methods. You can add and manage your payment methods in your account settings under "Payment Methods".
How do I track my order?
Once your order has been shipped, you'll receive a confirmation email with a tracking number. You can also view your order status and tracking information by going to "My Orders" in your profile. Click on the specific order to see detailed tracking updates.
What is your return policy?
Return policies vary by vendor. Most vendors accept returns within 7-30 days of purchase for unopened items. To initiate a return, go to "My Orders", select the order you want to return, and click "Request Return". The vendor will review your request and provide return instructions.
How do I become a vendor?
To become a vendor, go to your profile settings and click "Become a Vendor" or navigate to the vendor registration page. You'll need to provide business information, verify your identity, and set up your vendor profile. Once approved, you can start listing products and managing your store.
How do I update my profile information?
Go to your profile page and click on "Settings" or "Edit Profile". From there, you can update your display name, email, phone number, address, profile photo, and other personal information. Remember to save your changes before leaving the page.
How do I change my password?
In your account settings, navigate to "Account Information" and click on "Change Password". You'll need to enter your current password and then create a new password. Make sure your new password meets the security requirements (typically 8+ characters with a mix of letters, numbers, and symbols).
How do I contact a vendor directly?
You can contact vendors through the messaging system. Go to the vendor's profile page and click "Message" or find them in your messages. You can also contact vendors through their listed contact information if they've made it available on their profile.
What should I do if I receive a damaged or incorrect item?
If you receive a damaged or incorrect item, contact the vendor immediately through the messaging system or use the "Report an Issue" option in your order details. Take photos of the damaged item if possible. Most vendors will work with you to resolve the issue, which may include a replacement, refund, or partial credit.
How do I delete my account?
To delete your account, go to Settings > Help & Support. Scroll down to find the "Delete Account" option. You'll be asked to confirm your decision and may need to resolve any pending orders or disputes before the account can be permanently deleted. This action cannot be undone.
How do I report a problem or safety concern?
If you encounter a problem or safety concern, you can report it through several channels: use the "Report an Issue" option in your settings, contact customer service directly through the messaging system, or use the "Report" button on any user or vendor profile. We take all reports seriously and will investigate promptly.
